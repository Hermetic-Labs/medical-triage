Cookie Policy

Hermetic Labs, LLC · Last Updated: April 2026

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better experience and understand how visitors use our site.

✦ HALT Software Uses No Cookies

This policy applies only to the 7hermeticlabs.health website. The HALT triage software itself does not use cookies, tracking pixels, or any form of analytics. It runs entirely offline with zero telemetry.

Cookies We Use

Category Cookie Purpose Duration
Essential Session ID Required for website functionality Session
Analytics _ga, _gid Google Analytics — helps us understand site usage Up to 2 years
Payment __stripe_* Stripe fraud prevention for donation processing Session / 1 year
Hosting __cf_bm Cloudflare bot management (download delivery) 30 minutes

Your Choices

Accept or Reject

When you first visit our site, you can choose to accept or reject non-essential cookies through your browser settings.

Browser Settings

You can control cookies through your browser:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies
  • Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and Site Permissions

Opt-Out Links

Google Analytics: tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout

Impact of Disabling Cookies

Disabling essential cookies may prevent some website features from working properly. Disabling analytics cookies will not affect your ability to use the site or download the HALT software.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 12, 2026  |  Effective immediately

HALT collects zero personal data, sends zero telemetry, and makes zero network requests to Hermetic Labs or any third party. Your data never leaves your device.

1. Who We Are

HALT (Hermetic Anonymous Local Triage) is developed by Hermetic Labs. This privacy policy applies to the HALT desktop application distributed via the Microsoft Store and direct download.

Contact: Frontdesk@7hermeticlabs.com

2. Data We Collect

None. HALT is designed for air-gapped, offline operation in conflict zones and disaster response scenarios. The application:

  • Does not collect personal information
  • Does not transmit telemetry, analytics, or crash reports
  • Does not require an account or login
  • Does not use cookies, trackers, or advertising SDKs
  • Does not phone home to any server

3. Data Stored Locally

All data created within HALT — including patient records, triage assessments, chat messages, and translation caches — is stored exclusively on your local device. This includes:

  • Patient records: Stored in local JSON files within the application's data directory
  • Chat messages: Stored locally for team mesh communication over your local network
  • AI model data: All inference runs entirely on-device using locally stored models
  • User preferences: Language, theme, and settings stored in browser localStorage

You may delete all local data at any time by uninstalling the application or clearing the data directory.

4. Network Communication

HALT operates in two modes:

  • Fully offline (default): No network access required or attempted
  • Local mesh mode: When enabled by the user, HALT communicates over your local WiFi network (LAN) for team coordination. This traffic never leaves your local network and is not routed through any external server.

5. AI & Medical Triage

HALT includes an on-device AI model (MedGemma) for clinical triage assistance. All AI processing occurs locally — no queries are sent to cloud APIs. No patient data, prompts, or model outputs are transmitted externally.

6. Children's Privacy

HALT is a professional medical tool. It is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Third-Party Services

HALT uses zero third-party services, SDKs, or analytics platforms. The application is fully self-contained.

8. Microsoft Store

When you download HALT from the Microsoft Store, Microsoft may collect standard download and install telemetry per their own Privacy Statement. Hermetic Labs has no access to this data.

9. Changes to This Policy

If we ever modify this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Given HALT's zero-collection architecture, material changes are unlikely.
